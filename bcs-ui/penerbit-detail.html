Racing to the night
"Yoru ni Kakeru" (夜に駆ける, lit. "Racing into the Night") is the debut single by Japanese duo Yoasobi from their debut extended play, The Book (2021). It was released on December 15, 2019, through Sony Music Entertainment Japan. The song was based on Thanatos no Yūwaku (lit."An Invitation from Thanatos"), a short story by Mayo Hoshino, which was published on the creative writing social media Monogatary.com, and won the Sony Music Award, and the Grand Prize from Monocon 2019.
